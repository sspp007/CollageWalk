Rules of the Wall 📋

"Thoda pyaar, thoda care — bas itna hi chahiye." ❤️

01
Jo photo mummy ne dekh li toh ghar se nikaalna ho, woh yahan bhi mat daalna 😂🙏

Yeh wall sab ke liye hai — isse safe aur respectful rakhna humari zimmedari hai.

02
Akeli selfie se kaam nahi chalega — dost, yaar, koi bhi pakad le saath mein 📸❤️

Ek achhi memory woh hoti hai jisme koi aur bhi ho — apne logon ko frame mein le aao.

03
Kisi ki bhi photo bina permission ke mat daalna — friendship hai, court case nahi chahiye 💀⚖️

Doston ki photo unse pooch ke daalo — surprise unhe tab dena jab result aaye, yahan nahi.
